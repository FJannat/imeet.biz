    About Us

    Formed at the beginning of 1997 EdgeCom Digital are an award winning media agency with a technical focus. Seeing a gap in the market where technical implementation was concerned our initial focus was to give companies a different option when considering who to engage on digital projects. Since our inception we have grown steadily, mainly through word of mouth and the quality of our work. Today, as well as working directly with clients we also partner with many leading international creative agencies helping to form strategies and develop projects in the digital environment.
